The tournament witnessed memorable moments, such as Germany's stunning 7-1 victory over Brazil in the semifinals and Mario Götze's decisive goal in the final that secured Germany's fourth World Cup title.

The 2014 World Cup also left a lasting legacy in Brazil, as it showcased the country's passion for football and brought people from diverse backgrounds together to celebrate the beautiful game.
